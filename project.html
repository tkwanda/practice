Kisumu city

I love Kisumu city

Kisumu is a port city located in Kenya, Africa.It is the third largest city in Kenya. It is a beautiful city located right next to Lake Victoria. The climate in Kisumu is mostly hot and humid. Most of my childhood was spent in Kisumu and i always enjoyed the weather,the weekend afternoon swimming sessions with my friends and watching sunsets at the lakeside. Kisumu is definitely a perfect place for vacations.
